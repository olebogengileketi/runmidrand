ABOUT RUNMIDRAND

Where Fashion Meets Creativity & Community

Our Story

Runmidrand is a vibrant South African brand blending streetwear fashion with event services—bringing style, sound, and unforgettable memories to Midrand and beyond.

Founded in 2017 by Lebogang Charles Chiloane and Sibonelo Phakathi, our mission is to unite the community, create opportunities for youth, and deliver top-class experiences.

We aim to become Midrand's premier hub for fashion and multimedia—streamlining customer interaction, boosting brand visibility, and supporting young creatives across Gauteng.
